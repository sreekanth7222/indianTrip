Indian Mountains & Hill Stations

India’s mountains range from the mighty Himalayas to lush Western Ghats, offering snow peaks, tea gardens, trekking trails, and spiritual towns. Visitors come for adventure sports, fresh air, and stunning sunrise and sunset views.
From Ladakh’s high passes to the misty Nilgiris and tranquil Sikkim valleys, each range has its own culture, cuisine, and landscapes. Mountain getaways are perfect for hiking, camping, meditation retreats, or simply escaping the summer heat.
