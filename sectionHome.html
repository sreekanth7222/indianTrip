What international visitors say

“India’s mix of spiritual places and modern cities makes every trip feel completely different.”

“From the Himalayas to Goa, it’s easy to plan multi‑city itineraries and still discover something new each time.”

Tourists frequently describe India as a cultural melting pot, with 90% visiting for its history, festivals, and heritage sites like ancient monuments and temples. Travelers from places like Finland report overwhelmingly positive views on the old culture, beautiful sights, and great food, often calling it an "eye-opener" with immersive experiences. Reddit users and surveys emphasize tasty street food, friendly locals, chai culture, and value-for-money stays, with many planning return trips or recommendations
