About Indian Trip

Indian Trip is a simple travel website created to help people discover beautiful places across India — temples, waterfalls, beaches, hill stations, caves, and vibrant cities. It works like a small visual guide with clean sections and short descriptions, so anyone can quickly understand what a place is famous for and decide where to go next.

The website groups destinations into easy categories such as spiritual temple trips, nature spots like waterfalls and forests, coastal getaways, and city experiences full of culture and history. Each card shows the place name, location, and a short highlight, making it useful for students, families, and tourists who want a quick overview instead of long articles.

Meet the Founder

Sreekanth Ambavarapu

Founder & Developer, Indian Trip

Sreekanth Ambavarapu started Indian Trip as a simple project to combine a love for coding with a love for exploring India. The idea was to build a clean, beginner‑friendly travel website where users can quickly browse important places without getting lost in too much text.

As a developer, Sreekanth focuses on using modern web technologies and neat UI design so that every section — from temples and waterfalls to cities and mountains — feels smooth, responsive, and easy to use on both mobile and desktop. Indian Trip will continue to grow with more categories, better search, and smarter suggestions for travelers across India.
